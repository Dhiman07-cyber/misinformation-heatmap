Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data Collection

Misinformation Heatmap collects public digital signals for the purpose of identifying and verifying misinformation clusters. We do not collect private personal communications or non-public identifiable information.

2. Data Usage

Processed data is used solely to generate geographical heatmaps and regional signal reports. Aggregated data may be shared with verified research and government partners.

3. Regional Sovereignty

All data collected within India is stored and processed on local servers in compliance with national data protection regulations.
