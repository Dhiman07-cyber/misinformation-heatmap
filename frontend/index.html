Technology Stack

How Detection Works

Multi-layered AI analysis combining language models, fact-checking, and satellite verification.

🧠
IndicBERT NLP Engine

Advanced language embeddings specifically trained for Indian languages including Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, and 8 others — providing culturally-aware misinformation detection.

🤖
5-Model ML Ensemble

Five independently trained classifiers vote on each piece of content. Achieves 91% accuracy on our 940-example India-specific benchmark dataset with 0.973 ROC-AUC.

✅
Real-Time Fact Checking

Cross-references claims against Alt News, Boom Live, WebQoof and other verified Indian fact-checking organizations to validate breaking news stories.

🛰️
Satellite Verification

Geographic claims are validated using satellite imagery analysis (Google Earth Engine). Identifies anomalies between reported events and ground truth.

🗺️
Geographic Heatmap

Interactive SVG map of India showing real-time misinformation concentration by state. Click any region to drill into detailed event feeds and risk scores.

⚡
High-Volume Ingestion

Concurrent RSS fetching from 34+ Indian news outlets every 2 minutes. Processes 500+ articles per cycle with low-latency classification and state-wise aggregation.
