How It Works

Multi-Layer Detection Engine

Every article passes through 5 specialized models before a verdict is issued

🧠
IndicBERT NLP
State-of-the-art multilingual transformer trained on Indian languages including Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and 20+ more.
🔍
Fact-Check Engine
Cross-references against debunked claims from Alt News, Boom Live, The Quint WebQoof, and India Today Fact Check.
📡
Real-Time RSS Ingestion
Monitors 34+ Indian news sources including TOI, The Hindu, NDTV, Indian Express, and regional outlets every 2 minutes.
🗺️
Geographic Mapping
200+ city-to-state mappings identify the precise geographic origin of each misinformation event.
📊
Ensemble Classifier
5-model ensemble combining linguistic analysis, source credibility, ML classification, and cross-reference scoring.
🌐
GDELT Integration
Pulls India-specific news from the Global Database of Events — the world's largest open-source news index. Zero cost, unlimited volume.
